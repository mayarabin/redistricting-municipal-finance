# Cook Partisan Voting Index Access Instructions

The Cook Partisan Voting Index (Cook PVI) data used in this thesis are not included directly in this repository because they are proprietary and subject to third-party access restrictions.

The thesis uses Cook PVI scores for U.S. congressional districts from 1997 through 2025. These scores are used to measure congressional district partisan lean and to construct partisan alignment variables used in the empirical analysis.

Users seeking to replicate this portion of the analysis should obtain the relevant Cook PVI data directly from The Cook Political Report with Amy Walter:

https://www.cookpolitical.com/

The Cook Political Report provides PVI information, including congressional district-level partisan voting index scores. Access to historical or full district-level PVI data may require a subscription, institutional access, or direct access through The Cook Political Report.

The relevant data should include congressional district PVI scores covering the 105th Congress through the 119th Congress, corresponding to the period from 1997 through 2025.

After obtaining the data, users should place the Cook PVI files in this folder or otherwise adjust the file paths in the analysis scripts to point to the local location of the data.

The Cook PVI data are used only for internal analytical purposes in this project and are not redistributed as a standalone dataset.

Suggested citation:

The Cook Political Report with Amy Walter. Cook Partisan Voting Index. Retrieved from https://www.cookpolitical.com/
